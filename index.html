COSTA RICA

Where is Costa Rica?

Costa Rica is in Central america and its capital is San José. They have a tropical and subtropical climate, and there are only two seasons. The dry season: lasts from December to April, and the rainy season lasts from May to November. Costa Rica is mainly known for its popular vacation spots,amazing tourist attractions, and delicious foods If you are interested in going to Costa Rica, you will not regret going to theses beautiful places!

Costa Rican Native Food

Before you begin to travel, you should become familliar with native Costa Rican Foods.
1. Gallo Pinto
It is normally served during breakfast and it is mainly rice and beans with a side of eggs.

2. Chifrijo (fried pork with red beans}
Its name is the two main ingredients, and it is seerved with a base of rice and toppings like tomato and avocados

3.Ceviche
Costa Rican ceviche is served mainly with fish that is marinated in lime juice for a long time. sometimes you’ll find chuchecas (blood clams) and a mixture of chopped or minced onions, tomatoes, garlic, and cilantro
